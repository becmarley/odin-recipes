Tator Tot Casserole

Creamy, yet chunky. Thick, yet smooth. This potato soup is going to be the best part of your day. Below you will find the ingredients and steps to making your day that much better. Enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 16 oz frozen vegetables
  • 16 oz frozen tater tots
  • 1 10.5 oz can cream of chicken soup
  • Milk
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In a frying pan, brown the beef with the onion (chopped). Salt and pepper accordingly. Drain the fat.
  2. In a 9x13 baking dish, combine the two cans of soup, the frozen veggies (don't need to be thawed), and the beef/onion mix, and add a couple splashes of milk (probably about 1/4 to 1/2 cup).
  3. Bake it in the oven at 350 F for 25-30 minutes
  4. Take it out of the oven and add a layer of shredded cheese over it all. Crunchy onions too if that floats your boat.
  5. Put it back in the oven for another 10-15 minutes until the cheese is all melted
